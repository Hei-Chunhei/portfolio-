Intro

My name is Hei Chui, I have been working in the Fashion industry since 2000. I have accomplished many things in diverse types of companies from small high-end evening wear to mass-market corporations to contemporary RTW (Men and Womenswear). I blogged for fashion websites on trends and fashion shows, including running my own fashion lines in contemporary and junior’s jeans brand.

Among my design experiences, I have various backgrounds in production, product development, merchandising, and fashion photo shoots. In addition I have great knowledge about fabrics, trim designs, graphic and print design, and leather goods. I believe throughout the years hands on knowledge I would be a great asset to any company. If you have made all the way to the end, check out my work here.
